About Us

Welcome to S1-G3

S1_G3 launched into the real estate market in 2018 as a university project to help homeowners advertise their vacancies and renters find their perfect home. Our goal was to help all people rent their homes. S1_G3 has rapidly changed over submissions as our knowledge of the rental industry and available technology in Django has evolved. We strive to provide the most technologically advanced user friendly site available by constantly updating with new features geared towards renters and landlords. We want to be the most trusted source with the highest number of qualified listings. We also want to be the #1 online resource for landlords with our extensive library of rental documents provided free of charge. This project was done by :
